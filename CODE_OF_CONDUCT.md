# Code of Conduct

Version Watch follows the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but accept feedback when your words or actions create harm.
- Keep discussions focused on the project, its users, and verifiable technical work.
- Credit sources and contributors when building on their work.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Trolling, sustained disruption, or bad-faith arguments.
- Sexualized language or imagery in project spaces.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to `parrisdigital@gmail.com`. Security vulnerabilities should follow `SECURITY.md` instead of public issues.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block users, or take other reasonable action to keep the project safe and productive.
